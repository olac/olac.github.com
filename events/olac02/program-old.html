APPENDIX: ORIGINAL FOUR ACTIVITIES

OLAC Standards

  • presentations of the standards: the OLAC Metadata Set, the OLAC Process document, and the OLAC Protocol.
  • review and revision, approval

OLAC Vocabularies

  • linguistic type
  • language
  • rights
  • role
  • software: functionality, os, cpu, distribution, format, ...

Review

To give feedback to each participating archive on its use of metadata, to review the services on the OLAC and LINGUIST sites.

Proposals

To hear new proposals for working groups, encoding schemes, implementation notes and best practice recommendations, and position papers on work that still needs to be done.
